Automated Reports: Simplify Your Seed Trials with SeedSense

Seed trials fuel innovation for vegetable seed companies, but turning raw data into shareable reports often feels like a second job. Spreadsheets get copied into Word documents, reformatted for different stakeholders, and stuffed into bulky email attachments—only for someone to miss the file or get the wrong version. What if you could generate professional trial reports with a single click?

SeedSense makes reporting effortless with automated report-building. After creating and evaluating a trial, our platform generates trial maps and evaluation reports instantly, tailored for suppliers, farmers, or team members, and emails them as downloadable PDF links. No spreadsheets, no bloated emails—just clean, actionable insights that save time and keep everyone on the same page.

The Reporting Grind: Why It's Holding You Back

Imagine your team's just finished a lettuce trial in Yuma, scoring varieties for yield and disease resistance. The data's in a spreadsheet, but now you need to share it. You export to Word, adjust layouts, and create a supplier-specific version showing only their material. The farmer wants a trial map, so you start from scratch again. Hours later, you're emailing bulky PDFs, hoping they don't bounce or get lost in inboxes. Sound familiar?

Manual reporting creates real roadblocks:

  • Time Drain Formatting and emailing reports steals hours from trial analysis or planning.
  • Data Mishaps Copy-pasting risks errors, and sending the wrong report to a supplier can cause confusion.
  • Slow Sharing Delayed reports mean stakeholders wait longer to act, slowing down variety decisions.

SeedSense: Automated Reports, One Click Away

SeedSense eliminates the reporting grind with a cloud-based platform that automates the entire process. Log your trial evaluations—scores, photos, traits—and generate professional reports instantly. Here's how it works:

One-Click Report Generation

Create trial maps and evaluation reports instantly after evaluations, no manual formatting needed.

Tailored Stakeholder Reports

Cloud-Based Access

Empowering Your Stakeholders

SeedSense's automated reports deliver value to everyone involved in your trials:

  • Suppliers Receive concise reports on their material's performance.
  • Farmers Get full trial maps and evaluations to understand variety potential for their fields.
  • Teams Access detailed reports instantly, keeping breeders to sales aligned on trial outcomes.

The Impact: Faster, Smarter Trials

Automated reporting isn't just about convenience—it's about results:

  • Save Hours Generate and share reports in seconds, freeing your team for high-value work.
  • Boost Accuracy Automated data pulls eliminate manual errors, building trust with stakeholders.
  • Speed Up Decisions Instant reports help suppliers and farmers act faster, accelerating commercialization.
