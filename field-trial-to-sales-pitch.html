From Field Trial to Sales Pitch: How to Turn Performance Data into Deals

Your best salespeople aren't closing deals with brochures — they're closing them with proof. Here's how to turn your seed trial data into the most compelling sales tool your team has ever had.

5 min read · April 2025 · SeedSense Team

Most seed companies invest enormous resources in field trials — hundreds of plots, dozens of varieties, entire seasons of painstaking work. Yet when the sales team walks into a distributor meeting, they're often armed with glossy brochures and general claims. The data that could win the deal is buried in a spreadsheet back at the office.

That disconnect is costing you sales.

The companies closing the most deals aren't just running better trials — they're weaponizing their trial data. They walk into every sales conversation with location-specific performance reports, side-by-side variety comparisons, and yield numbers that speak directly to what each buyer cares about. And with SeedSense, they produce those materials in minutes, not weeks.

Why Trial Data Is Your Most Persuasive Sales Tool

A sales rep saying "our variety outperforms the competition" is a claim. A sales rep handing over a PDF with actual yield comparisons from trials run 30 miles from the distributor's territory is evidence.

The difference matters enormously. Distributors and buyers are sophisticated — they've heard every claim. What moves them is specificity: data from their region, their crop type, their growing conditions.

  • Localized proof beats generic claims — Performance data from nearby trials is far more convincing than broad national averages.
  • Numbers reduce objections — When you can show germination rates, yield data, and disease resistance scores side by side, price objections shrink.
  • Comparisons build buyer confidence — Side-by-side variety data helps buyers self-select the right product without feeling pressured.
  • Real-time results create urgency — When a buyer sees a top-performing variety trending upward across multiple trial sites, they act before inventory runs out.

The Problem: Getting the Data Into the Sales Room

Here's where most seed companies stumble. The data exists — it's just locked away. Field reps collect it in notebooks or scattered spreadsheets. Researchers compile it weeks after harvest. By the time it reaches the sales team, the season is over or the meeting has already passed.

Common Bottlenecks

  • Siloed spreadsheets — Trial results live in files only the research team can access or interpret.
  • Manual report formatting — Compiling a distributor-ready report takes hours of copying and pasting.
  • No real-time visibility — Sales reps have no way to see which varieties are performing best while the season is still active.
  • Inconsistent data — When field teams record data differently, it's hard to present results with confidence.

How SeedSense Closes the Gap

This is exactly the problem SeedSense was built to solve. The platform connects your field teams, your trial data, and your sales conversations — so performance evidence gets into the right hands at the right moment.

Real-Time Field Data

As field reps evaluate plots, results are captured instantly in SeedSense — not later in a spreadsheet. Sales teams can monitor variety performance while the trial season is still active, so no opportunity is missed.

One-Click Professional Reports

Side-by-Side Variety Comparisons

Location-Specific Results

Because SeedSense tracks GPS coordinates for every trial, your sales team can present data from plots geographically close to each prospect. That's the most persuasive kind of proof there is.

A Sales Conversation, Transformed

Before SeedSense, a rep preparing for a distributor meeting might spend an afternoon digging through spreadsheets, copy-pasting numbers into a presentation, and hoping the data is current. The result is a report that arrives late, looks rough, and may already be out of date.

With SeedSense, the morning of that same meeting the rep:

  • Filters trial results by region and target variety
  • Exports a formatted PDF comparison report in one click
  • Walks in with localized, current, professionally presented performance data

The conversation shifts from "trust us, it works" to "here's exactly how it performed 20 miles from your warehouse."

That's the difference between a maybe and a signed order.
