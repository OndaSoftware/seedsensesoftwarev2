Discover the Advanced Features of SeedSense

SeedSense provides the tools you need to manage trials efficiently, collect accurate data, and make smarter decisions.

Offline-First Mobile App

Access your trials and record data even in areas with no connectivity. SeedSense's mobile app ensures uninterrupted work in the field.

  • Full functionality without an internet connection.
  • Automatic syncing when you're back online.
  • Available on iOS and Android devices.

Integrated GPS and Mapping

Visualize your trial locations with precision and share maps with your team for improved collaboration and navigation.

  • Track trial locations accurately.
  • Generate clear maps for team members.
  • Simplify navigation to remote trial sites.

Thorough Grading and Evaluation Options

Grade and evaluate trials with precision using customizable templates and detailed scoring tools.

  • Design templates to suit your specific needs.
  • Compare performance traits effortlessly.
  • Log observations and photos in real time.

Dynamic Data Management

Keep all your trial data organized and accessible, with powerful tools for filtering and retrieval.

  • Store detailed records by trial, crop, or supplier.
  • Retrieve data quickly with advanced filters.
  • Ensure data consistency and accuracy.

Custom Reports and Trial Maps

Generate trial maps and evaluation reports that can be shared internally or externally with key stakeholders.

  • Create detailed trial maps after planting.
  • Generate evaluation reports post-assessment.
  • Email reports to farmers, suppliers, production teams, and sales teams.
