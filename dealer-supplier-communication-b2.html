When Good Trials Go Quiet: Solving the Supplier-Dealer Data Gap

Why seed companies are missing critical trial insights — and how modern software can fix broken communication channels between distributors and breeding companies.

4 min read · April 2025 · SeedSense Team

In our conversations with seed supplier companies — from solo breeders to global producers — one frustration keeps surfacing: "We never get the trial data from our dealer's product development teams."

But here's the twist: it's not that seed dealers are withholding information. As one product manager told us: "Our distributors want to share results, but they're stuck using spreadsheets and emails. By the time we get any data, it's outdated or incomplete."

Why Traditional Methods Fail

The current process often looks like this:

  • Dealer PD teams snap phone photos (if they remember)
  • Trial notes are taken on paper or in spreadsheets
  • Suppliers piece together Excel sheets from multiple sources
  • Weeks pass before anyone sees the full picture

This fragmented workflow means valuable performance data gets trapped in silos — or lost entirely before it reaches the people who need it most.

How SeedSense Changes the Game

We built SeedSense to solve these exact pain points. Here's what the platform enables across the supplier-dealer relationship:

Field-Ready Data Capture

Distributors can record trial evaluations directly in the field from any device

Instant Trial Updates

Automatically share GPS-mapped trial locations with suppliers the moment planting is complete

Real-Time Evaluation Sharing

Suppliers receive standardized PDF reports with one click immediately after field assessments

Auto-Generated Reports

Standardized reports eliminate manual compilation and reduce errors

Real-Time Monitoring

Track trial progress across multiple locations from any device

Custom Data Exports (Coming Soon)

Filter and share specific variety performance data across regions directly with breeding teams

"With SeedSense, our suppliers get trial updates as fast as we do. No more asking for photos or deciphering handwritten notes months after we wrapped up the season." — Donavin Buck, Sales and Product Development, Seedway
