Where's Your Trial Data? How to Keep It All in One Place

Learn how SeedSense centralizes trial data, ensuring no insight is lost. From real-time updates to shareable reports, streamline your trials and boost efficiency for vegetable seed companies.

5 min read · 2025 · SeedSense Team

Seed companies invest enormous resources in trials — labor, land, inputs, and expertise. But too often, the insights from those trials never fully reach the teams that need them. Data ends up scattered across personal spreadsheets, email threads, and field notebooks, making it nearly impossible to see the complete picture.

The Problem: Trial Data That's Out of Sight and Out of Mind

Picture a spinach trial running across three locations with separate regional teams. Team A has their evaluations in a local spreadsheet. Team B emailed results to a product manager who's traveling. Team C posted photos in a group chat. When it's time to make a variety decision, no one has the full story — and a promising variety could slip through the cracks.

  • Missed Opportunities — A promising variety's traits go unnoticed because the trial wasn't tracked or shared properly.
  • Wasted Resources — Time, money, and effort go into trials that end up as unused data on a spreadsheet, never informing decisions.
  • Slow Decisions — Without a clear view of all trial data, selecting top varieties drags on, delaying commercialization.

SeedSense: Your Single Source of Truth

SeedSense gives your entire organization one place where all trial data lives — updated in real time, accessible from any device, and organized for action.

Centralized Storage

All trial records, evaluations, GPS data, and photos stored in one platform — no more hunting across folders and inboxes

Real-Time Updates

Field teams log data directly from their device and it's instantly visible to the entire organization

Organized for Action

Filter by variety, location, season, or team to find exactly what you need when you need it

Cloud Access

Access your complete trial history from any device — office, field, or on the road

Teamwork Without the Headaches

Centralized data isn't just about storage — it's about enabling your team to work together effectively, even across locations and time zones.

  • Task Assignments — Assign planting, evaluation, and harvest tasks to team members and track completion in one place.
  • Shareable Reports — Generate and share trial summaries instantly with suppliers, internal teams, or growers.
  • Cloud Access — Everyone on the team sees the same data, eliminating version conflicts and missed updates.

The Payoff: Faster Decisions, Better Varieties

  • Speed — Make variety decisions weeks faster when all data is visible in one place.
  • Accuracy — Reduce errors from manual data transfer and ensure every evaluation gets recorded.
  • Impact — Get more value from every trial dollar invested by ensuring insights actually reach decision-makers.

Stop Chasing Data. Start Using It.

The best trial programs aren't the ones with the most data — they're the ones that can actually find and use it. SeedSense makes sure every piece of field intelligence gets captured, organized, and put to work.
